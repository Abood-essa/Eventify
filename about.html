About Us

Eventify is a user-friendly platform that connects event organizers and attendees in one centralized space. Developed by students at The Hashemite University, it was created to solve the problem of poor event turnout due to scattered communication in places like Jordan.

With tools for event creation, ticketing, and community engagement, Eventify makes organizing and discovering events simple and secure. Built on a scalable .NET backend with a responsive design, it's a smart solution for building stronger, more connected communities.

What We Offer?

  • Event Creation Tools – Easily create, manage, and publish events with customizable details.
  • Smart Event Discovery – Search and filter events by category, location, and preferences.
  • Secure Ticketing – Offer free or paid tickets with a smooth, secure checkout process.
  • Community Engagement – Build and join event communities with feedback
    and ratings.
  • Admin Dashboard – Manage users, monitor platform activity, and send announcements.

Who We Are?

We are a team of Software Engineering students from The Hashemite University, driven by a shared vision to improve how events are organized and experienced in our communities.
